About

What is FoxPanel?

FoxPanel is the ultimate solution for turning your most cherished photos into timeless, high-quality handmade photo panels. Each piece is crafted with precision, featuring a glossy, durable finish and our signature no-nails sticker mounting—so you can install in seconds without damaging your walls.

Upload your pictures, choose the size and style, preview instantly, and get them delivered to your doorstep. From single hero shots to elegant gallery walls, FoxPanel makes your memories look museum-ready.

  • Handmade finish with premium lamination
  • 1.5 cm depth, lightweight & ready to stick
  • Clean, borderless look—no frames required
  • Free delivery on bulk orders across UAE
