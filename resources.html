Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing/shipping address.
Order Information: Photos or designs provided for custom products, product preferences, and order history.
Payment Information: Payment method details processed through secure third-party gateways.
Technical Information: Device type, browser, IP address, and cookies when you use our website.

How We Use Your Information

Process and deliver your orders; communicate regarding orders, updates and promotions; improve our products, services, and customer experience; handle customer support requests; and ensure compliance with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in the following cases: Service Providers (courier partners, payment gateways, IT support); Legal Compliance (when required by law or to protect our legal rights); and Business Transfers (merger, acquisition, or restructuring).

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, opt-out of receiving promotional messages, and withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit website functionality.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised version will be posted on our website with an updated “Effective Date.”
